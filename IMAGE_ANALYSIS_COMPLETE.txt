Step 1: User uploads floor plan image (PNG, JPG, PDF)
   ↓
Step 2: Image converted to base64
   ↓
Step 3: Sent to Python Flask backend (analyze_image.py on port 5001)
   ↓
Step 4: Image analyzed (dimensions, features extracted)
   ↓
Step 5: ML model processes image features
   ↓
Step 6: Calculate 5 elements (Fire, Water, Earth, Air, Space)
   ↓
Step 7: Generate 2D matplotlib visualization (Bar + Radar charts)
   ↓
Step 8: Calculate Vastu Score = (ML × 40%) + (Elements Average × 60%)
   ↓
Step 9: Return visualization + scores + recommendations
   ↓
Step 10: Display on results page with animated charts
